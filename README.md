## 🚀 Mission
To replace extractive, fuel-dependent infrastructure with regenerative, passive thermodynamic loops. We engineer porous, carbon-doped concrete that acts as a **Volumetric Blackbody**, capturing solar energy and driving cooling/heating cycles with **zero electricity**.

### Key Innovations
- **Zero-Input Thermal Loops:** PTTR → ∞ (No fans, no pumps).
- **Volumetric Blackbody Absorption:** 98% solar spectrum capture via activated carbon doping.
- **Proof-of-Physical-Work (PoPW):** Token rewards for verified physical output.
- **21-Day Wet Cure Protocol:** Critical for structural integrity.

## 🛠️ How to Build (Phase 0)
1. **Source Materials:** Cement, Sand, Activated Carbon, Xanthan Gum, Dawn Dish Soap.
2. **Mix:** Follow the "Foam Solution" protocol in the Whitepaper.
3. **Pour:** Use cardboard formwork.
4. **Cure:** Keep wet for 21 days. **DO NOT SKIP.**
5. **Test:** Verify thermal delta ($\Delta T$) vs ambient.
